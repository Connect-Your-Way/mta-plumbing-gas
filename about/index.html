About us

Canberra's plumbing and gas team.

Local plumbers and gasfitters looking after homes and businesses across the ACT.

MTA Plumbing & Gas is a Canberra-based plumbing and gasfitting business serving customers right across the ACT. We handle the full range of plumbing and gas work — from hot water systems and gas fitting to blocked drains, leaks and general repairs — for both residential and commercial clients.

We know plumbing problems rarely happen at a convenient time, which is why we operate around the clock and take emergency call-outs. Whatever the job, we turn up, tell you straight what's going on, and get it sorted.
